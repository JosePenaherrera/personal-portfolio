My Role
This project took place between August 2017 and January 2018. I led this project as a Product Designer alongside a Product Manager, two designers from our Romania office and one from our India office. I was responsible for the research, information architecture, interaction design, visual design and branding concepts for the Cross-Platform Proposal Composer — a web‑based application for Cadent's product database.
The Design Challenge

Enabling an engaging and productive experience

Our client approached us with a primary objective — to redesign the current planning and proposal creation tool. The goal was to improve sales team efficiency and overall experience and to allow for self-service by the client in future.

The Discovery

What we've done

The backbone of our work was based on a human-centered design approach. We learned directly from the people we were designing for as we immerse in their lives and come to deeply understand their needs.

I lead the research work, producing all major deliverables of this phase and presenting these to the client.

Interaction Desing Persona - The focus of this persona was to identify the behavioral patterns and understanding why, how, and in what context our users interact with their environment. It emphasized the key tasks a person was motivated to accomplish, and the common scenarios they find themselves in.

The initial insights from the behavioral patterns didn't complete the whole picture of our users. Therefore, I conducted a workshop to map the experience. This strategic process helped to build knowledge and consensus across the organization, and the map helped build a seamless experience.

The workshop was structured using the four steps of experience mapping:

  • Uncover the truth through customer behavior and interactions across channels and touchpoints
  • Chart the course in order to obtain key insights
  • Tell the story visually to create empathy and understanding, and
  • Use the map to generate solutions and better customer experiences.
View a full resolution of the experience map

Using the structure provided by the map, we identified the opportunities for addressing customer pain points at each stage of the user journey. Our prioritization was done according to immediate business needs and user value.

We concentrated on optimizing the users' ability to accomplish the tasks with the least amount of friction during the building plan stage. As a result we worked on various iterations of the critical path our user followed through the application.

Reframing the Problem

Optimizing a plan to maximize brand reach

By defining themes and insights, we identified problem areas that pose challenges to the users we’re designing for. We reframed our insights statements as How Might We questions to turn those challenges into opportunities for design.

How might we help users specify parameters to find campaigns that meet their client's goals and budget?

How might we help users refine a campaign that is already optimized?

How might we make it easy for users to duplicate a campaign so they don't start from scratch?

How might we make it easy and quick for users to immediately identify how a campaign is performing?

The Solution

User Interface Design

With the confidence that our design solution works for the users, and the knowledge that changes will likely occur as the product matures and evolves. We were able to successfully deliver an experience that allows users to easily create and manage campaign/plans, monitor their individual and group performance, identify areas of weakness, and take action by making real-time changes on the campaigns/plans in the appropriate manner.

I worked with another Senior Designer to define the visual design and branding concepts. The designed interface is clean and simple, devoid of any distractions in order to maximize the user’s focus while managing their proposals. A palette of blues and whites also served to calm the user and keep them focused. The finalization of the visual design work was completed by our design members in Romania and India.

The campaign creation feature offers a unique and engaging interface. It is an easy to use web application that enables full control to drive growth on sales.

A robust editing functionality that allows users to optimize media across any dimensions.

The data-driven linear recommendations help the users to make the right adjustments to increase reach and frequency, to reduce the overall CPM and CPP (methods of evaluating media efficiency).

I initiated a themeable Design System with elements and components that can be reused in different combinations. The Design System allows you to manage design at scale.
