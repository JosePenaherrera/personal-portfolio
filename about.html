Guiding Principles
A set of considerations that form the basis of how to design
Design for non-humans

Design for humans but also for non-humans. In the same way that we design to anticipate human needs, we will have to design to anticipate our planet's needs.

Evaluate impact

Research to evaluate our development's impact on all human and environmental threats to see how you can adapt our product to be less nocive.

Don't hurt anyone

Protect people and the environment to create positive experiences. At the very least, make sure we don't cause any pain - make our life goal not add to it.

Make things simple

The things we create should be easy to use, easy to learn, easy to find, and easy to adapt. Intuition occurs outside of conscious reasoning in people's minds.
